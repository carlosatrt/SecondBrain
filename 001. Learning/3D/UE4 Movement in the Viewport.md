# Movement in the UE4 viewport
Tags: #UnrealEngine
Date: 14-08-2020

---
- Right Mouse Boton (RMB)
> Move forward: RMB + ↑   
> Move backwards: RMB + ↓
> Look left: RMB + ←
> Look right: RMB + →
- Left Mouse Boton (LMB)
> Look up:  LMB + ↑
> Look down: LMB + ↓
> Look left: LMB + ←
> Look right: LMB + →
- Middle Mouse Boton (MMB/Scroll)
> Move up: MMB +  ↑
> Move down: MMB + ↓
> Move left: MMB + ←
> Move right: MMB + →
> Zoom(-): Scroll Back
>  Zoom(+): Scroll forward
- Extra movements
> Move forward: RMB/LMB + W
> Move backwards: RMB/LMB + S
> Move left: RMB/LMB + A
> Move right: RMB/LMB + D
> Move up: RMB/LMB + Q
> Move doen: RMB/LMB + E
> Change speed of movement fast/slow: RMB/LMB + Scroll
